## 1. SHOW SETUP

### 1.1 Show Types (F.4)

| Show Type | Description | Eligibility | Regulation |
|-----------|------------|-------------|------------|
| **Championship** | Highest level; RKC Challenge Certificates offered | Open to all exhibitors (except where Board-approved qualification required) | F(1) |
| **Open** | Open to all registered pedigree dogs | All exhibitors | F(1) |
| **Premier Open** | Open show on group system, 50%+ breeds separately classified per group, min 2 classes per breed | Crufts qualification for 1st-4th in group/puppy group | F(1) |
| **Limited** | Entry restricted to members of society or residents of specified area | Members/residents only; dogs that have won a CC or obtained Champion title ineligible | F(1) |
| **Matches** | Elimination one-against-one competition, intra-club or inter-club | Members of competing societies; 4 months+ age; max 12 per society per year | F(2) |
| **Companion** | Informal fundraising events at fetes/charity days | Registered and unregistered dogs eligible; crossbreeds welcome | F(3) |

**Source:** https://www.royalkennelclub.com/activities/heritage-sports/dog-showing/dog-show-secretary-information/
**Mandatory** | All show types

> **Key rule (F.4):** Dogs which have won a Challenge Certificate, five Reserve Challenge Certificates (under different judges) [effective 2026], or obtained any award counting towards Champion under rules of any governing body recognised by RKC (e.g. CACIB, CAC, Green Star) are not eligible for entry at Limited Shows, except for the open class at breed club limited shows for Imported Register Breeds and breeds not allocated championship status in the UK.

> **Partnership shows:** Defined as shows where the host club and other clubs share a venue and other facilities to allow exhibitors the opportunity to exhibit at each show. Require separate licences. (F(1))

### 1.2 Licence Application (F.8 / F(1).1)

| Requirement | Detail | Status |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| Application deadline | At least 12 months before proposed show date | **Mandatory** |
| Licence fee | Must be paid with application | **Mandatory** |
| Licence fee amount | GBP 45 (open shows and premier open shows) | **Mandatory** |
| Validity | Only valid if current public liability insurance held on day of event | **Mandatory** |
| Board power | Board may grant, withhold, suspend or cancel any show licence | **Mandatory** |
| New societies restriction (Year 1) | One limited show only | **Mandatory** |
| New societies restriction (Year 2) | Two limited shows only | **Mandatory** |
| New societies (Year 3+) | May apply for open shows with justification including entry numbers | **Mandatory** |
| General canine societies | Maximum two open shows annually (second requires average class entry of 4+ dogs at two most recent shows, 3.5 for NI) | **Mandatory** |
| Breed clubs | Normally two shows yearly (third possible if fewer than four clubs serve the breed) | **Convention** |
| Online application | Via club portal | **Mandatory** |

**Source:** https://www.royalkennelclub.com/activities/heritage-sports/dog-showing/dog-show-secretary-information/, F Regs 2025 p.6
**Applicable:** All show types

### 1.5 Facilities (F(1).3)

| Requirement | Detail | Status |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| Judging rings | Appropriate to number of exhibits/breeds; not less than 20ft (6.15m) wide; not less than 500 sq ft (45 sq m) clear floor space | **Mandatory** |
| Ring enclosure | Must be enclosed by seats or otherwise; persons on seats not within enclosure | **Mandatory** |
| Wet weather (outdoor) | Adequate alternative rings under satisfactory cover; plans available at secretary's office | **Mandatory** - championship/open on group system |
| Wet weather (indoor) | Not required at breed club championship, breed club open, general canine open, and limited shows | **Convention** |
| Gangways (benches both sides) | Minimum 6ft (1.85m) wide unobstructed | **Mandatory** |
| Gangways (ring/benches one side) | Minimum 3ft (0.92m) wide unobstructed | **Mandatory** |
| Judging tables | For breeds customarily judged on table: suitable size, stable construction, rubber mat or non-slip surface | **Mandatory** |
| Award boards | Group/general championship: board showing awards, clearly displayed after each class | **Mandatory** |
| Award area | Breed club championship, open, limited: area for display of awards | **Mandatory** |
| Grooming area | Designated area; use of chalk, powders or similar substances prohibited within show precincts | **Mandatory** |
| Exercise areas | Clearly defined | **Mandatory** |
| Cages/pens/trolleys | Must not obstruct gangways | **Mandatory** |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 p.6-7
**Applicable:** Championship, open, limited shows

### 1.6 Benching (F(1).4)

> **2026 CHANGE:** Benching is now OPTIONAL. Previously mandatory, benching "may" be provided (changed from "must"). If provided, the rules below apply.

| Requirement | Detail | Status |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| Benching materials | Easily cleaned and disinfected; reasonable comfort and security; arrangements for secure fastening | **Mandatory** (if provided) |
| Interference | No person may interfere with benching or remove partitions between pens | **Mandatory** |
| Bench cards | White, max 25.5cm x 2.7cm (10" x 5"), black lettering max 2.5cm (1") height | **Mandatory** (if benched) |
| Bench card content | "Not for Competition" with dog name, OR kennel name + owner's name/address/phone + printed awards including CCs at that show | **Mandatory** (if benched) |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 p.7, 2026 amendments PDF
**Applicable:** Championship, open, limited shows

| Requirement | Detail | Status |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| Judge-linked sponsorship | No sponsorship/donation may be conditional on a specified person being appointed judge, EXCEPT by registered breed societies | **Mandatory** |

## 2. ENTRIES

### 2.1 Eligibility (F.1)

| Rule | Detail | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| Minimum age for exhibition | Four calendar months on first day of show | **Mandatory** |
| Minimum age for competition | Six calendar months on first day of show | **Mandatory** |
| NFC entries | Societies may accept at discretion for dogs aged 12 weeks+ (effective 2026: dogs aged 12 weeks+; previously 4 months) | **Discretionary** |
| Registration | Must be registered at RKC for Classification and Registration B (except companion shows) | **Mandatory** |
| Breed standard | Only breeds with a published breed standard or interim breed standard eligible | **Mandatory** |
| Savage dogs | Board may declare ineligible if of savage disposition | **Mandatory** |
| Disqualified persons | Cannot take part in any RKC-licensed show | **Mandatory** |
| Handler contact details | Must be available and provided on request in investigations (effective 2025) | **Mandatory** |
| Surgical alterations | Dogs with surgical alterations require "Permission to Show" form with veterinary report | **Mandatory** |
| Docked dogs | Cannot compete at events where public admitted on payment of fee (most UK jurisdictions), with exemptions for legally docked working breeds | **Mandatory** |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 p.1, 2025/2026 amendments
**Applicable:** All show types

### 2.2 Entry Process (F(1).8)

| Rule | Detail | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| Entry closing | Must close at time indicated in schedule; at latest 7th day before show opens | **Mandatory** |
| Online vs postal | Societies have discretion to accept online only, postal only, or both; schedule must clearly state which | **Mandatory** |
| Entry form requirement | Separate application for each exhibitor; entry declaration must be accepted | **Mandatory** |
| Joint ownership | If dog in partnership, each partner must be member of society at time of entry (for members' specials/classes) | **Mandatory** |
| Judge conflict | No entry may be made at any show under a judge of any dog where said judge or immediate household/family has owned, handled, boarded or prepared such dog within 12 months prior to show | **Mandatory** |
| Judge-bred dogs | No entry under a judge who has bred the dog (does not apply to emergency appointments) | **Mandatory** |
| Registration requirement | Dog must be registered in name of person making entry (or registration/transfer applied for) | **Mandatory** |
| NAF/TAF/CNAF | Allowed when registration applications pending | **Permitted** |
| Overseas dogs (ATC) | Must have RKC-issued Authority to Compete number; entries without must be returned | **Mandatory** |
| Payment | Must be made in advance (except companion shows — payment on day) | **Mandatory** |
| Late postal entries | If received by post bearing correct postmark of closing date or before, must be accepted | **Mandatory** |
| Lost-in-post claims | If substantiated by postal evidence, must be accepted on show day; RKC will request documentary evidence post-show | **Mandatory** |
| Entry extensions | Permitted minimum 14 days before show; RKC must be informed in writing | **Permitted** |
| Entry fee refunds | RKC does not regulate entry fee costs or refunds — this is between society and exhibitor | **Convention** |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 pp.11-12, secretary information page
**Applicable:** Championship, open, limited shows

### 2.3 Not for Competition (NFC) Entries

| Rule | Detail | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| Minimum age | 12 weeks and over (effective 2026; previously 4 months) | **Mandatory** |
| Society discretion | Schedule must state whether NFC entries accepted or not | **Mandatory** |
| Details | Must be recorded on entry form; must be RKC registered or on register | **Mandatory** |
| NFC in schedule | Must appear either within schedule or on reverse of entry form | **Mandatory** |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 p.11, 2026 amendments
**Applicable:** All licensed shows

### 2.4 Withdrawal from Competition (F(1).19)

| Rule | Detail | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| Non-exhibition liability | If admitted but not exhibited in all entered classes and not brought to ring when required by judge: liable to disqualification at Board's discretion | **Mandatory** |
| Permitted withdrawal (a) | Per entry extension provisions (F(1).8.d) | **Permitted** |
| Permitted withdrawal (b) | When scheduled judge is changed and notice given: may withdraw from classes under substitute judge by giving notice to secretary before judging commences | **Permitted** |
| Permitted withdrawal (c) | When exhibitor or representative applies to judge: dog must not compete further at that show | **Permitted** |
| Remaining unbeaten | When declared Best of Breed to remain unbeaten: at exhibitor's discretion | **Permitted** |
| Illness at show | Application to show secretary; if thought necessary, dog examined by vet; reported to RKC within 7 days of close | **Mandatory** |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 pp.16-17
**Applicable:** Championship, open, limited shows

### 2.5 Transfer Classes (F(1).8.m)

| Rule | Detail | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| Ineligible entry | If dog ineligible for a class, exhibitor may choose withdrawal or transfer | **Permitted** |
| Transfer: wrong breed/colour/sex/weight/height | Secretary transfers to equivalent class or Open Class if no equivalent exists | **Mandatory** |
| Transfer: wrong age class | If entered in Minor Puppy/Puppy/Junior but over age, transferred to Puppy/Junior/Yearling Class respectively, or Open Class | **Mandatory** |
| Late arrival & missed class | If only class entered, dog may not be transferred | **Mandatory** |
| Any other transfer | Secretary transfers to Open Class for correct breed/colour/sex/weight/height | **Mandatory** |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 pp.12-13
**Applicable:** Championship, open, limited shows

## 4. SHOW DAY

### 4.1 Documentation Required at Show (F.9)

| Document | Status |
|----------|--------|
| The licence for the show | **Mandatory** |

### 4.2 Welfare & Conduct

| Rule | Detail | Regulation | Status |
|------|--------|------------|--------|
| Welfare of dogs | Exhibitors must take all reasonable steps for dog's needs; must not knowingly put health/welfare at risk | F.2 | **Mandatory** |
| Prohibited collars | Pinch collars, electronic shock collars, prong collars not permitted at venue or within precincts | F.2 | **Mandatory** |
| No punitive handling | No person shall carry out punitive correction or harsh handling at any time at licensed venue including environs, car/caravan parks | F.11 | **Mandatory** |
| Dog control | Owner/exhibitor/handler must ensure dog kept under proper control at all times at venue and environs | F.11 | **Mandatory** |
| Feet must touch ground | Not acceptable to handle a dog causing feet not to touch ground when on the move | F.11 | **Mandatory** |
| Table lifting | Picking up by neck and/or tail when lifting to/from tables is prohibited | F.11 | **Mandatory** |
| Infectious disease | No dog suffering from or exposed to contagious/infectious disease in preceding 21 days may be exhibited | F.15 | **Mandatory** |
| Mating | Mating of bitches within show precincts is forbidden | F.13 | **Mandatory** |
| Chalking/powdering | Prohibited within precincts (exception: water) | F(1).3.g | **Mandatory** |
| Firearms & sound amplifiers | Prohibited except as authorised by management | F(1).17 | **Mandatory** |
| Cooking stoves | Prohibited in proximity of tenting/benching except as authorised | F(1).18 | **Mandatory** |
| Other animals | No animal other than an exhibit shall be brought into precincts (exceptions: assistance dogs, health testing dogs, educational/instructional dogs by Board permission) | F(1).10 | **Mandatory** |
| Spectators' dogs | General/group championship: society must apply to Board for permission to admit | F(1).10 | **Mandatory** |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 pp.1-2, 4-5, 15-16
**Applicable:** All licensed shows

### 4.3 Exhibition of Dogs (F(1).16)

> **2026 CHANGE:** Benching-related exhibition rules deleted. Dogs no longer required to be present on benches at all times (benching now optional).

| Rule | Detail | Status |
|------|--------|--------|

| Gangway obstruction | Prohibited | **Mandatory** |
| Only actual exhibits in ring | No other dogs allowed in judging ring during judging | **Mandatory** |
| Awards in ring | Printed awards may not be displayed in ring except during Groups and BIS | **Mandatory** |
| Double handling | Attracting attention of exhibits by any method from outside the ring is prohibited | **Mandatory** |

| No impugning decisions | No competitor shall impugn decision of judge(s) | **Mandatory** |

**Source:** F Regs 2025 pp.15-16
**Applicable:** Championship, open, limited shows

### 4.5 Vet Health Checks (Breed Watch Category 3)

|------|--------|--------|

### 5.4 Cancellation

#### Win-Based Classes

| Class | Definition |
|-------|-----------|
| **Novice** | Dogs which have not won a CC/CACIB/CAC/Green Star or 3+ first prizes at open/championship shows (excluding baby puppy, minor puppy, puppy, special puppy classes) |
| **Undergraduate** | Dogs which have not won a CC/CACIB/CAC/Green Star or 3+ first prizes at championship shows in Undergraduate, Graduate, Post Graduate, Minor Limit, Mid Limit, Limit, or Open classes where CCs offered (Minor Puppy, Special Minor Puppy, Puppy, Special Puppy excepted) — **clarified 2025** |
| **Graduate** | Dogs which have not won a CC/CACIB/CAC/Green Star or 4+ first prizes at championship shows in Graduate, Post Graduate, Minor Limit, Mid Limit, Limit, or Open classes |
| **Post Graduate** | Dogs which have not won a CC or 5+ first prizes at championship shows in Post Graduate, Minor Limit, Mid Limit, Limit, or Open classes |
| **Minor Limit** | Dogs which have not won a CC or 2+ first prizes in all at championship shows in Minor Limit, Mid Limit, Limit, or Open classes |
| **Mid Limit** | Dogs which have not won a CC or 3+ first prizes in all at championship shows in Mid Limit, Limit, or Open classes |
| **Limit** | Dogs which have not become Show Champions under RKC regulations or rules of any recognised body; not won 3+ CACIB/CAC/Green Stars; not won 7+ first prizes at championship shows in Limit or Open classes where CCs offered |
| **Open** | All dogs of the breed for which the class is provided and eligible for entry at the show |

#### Important Notes on Win Counting

- In estimating awards won, all wins up to and including the 7th day before the first closing date count for any class
- Wins in variety classes do NOT count for entry in breed classes
- First prizes in classes defined as "Special" do not count towards eligibility
- Wins in sweepstake classes do not count

| Toward Champion | Do NOT count toward stud book numbers or Junior Warrant points | **Mandatory** |

### 11.3 Best of Breed

| Rule | Detail | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| Declaration | Where breed is separately classified, Best of Breed may be declared from those dogs which have received a first prize in a breed class at the show | **Mandatory** |
| Separate sex classes | Where separate classes per sex: Best of each Sex must be declared | **Mandatory** |
| JW Points | 3 points at CC championship shows (only if no other JW points gained from class wins/BOS at same show) | **Process** |

### 11.4 Junior Warrant (JW)

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Age | 6-18 months |
| Points needed | 25 total |
| Minimum from CC championship | 3 points |
| Minimum from open/non-CC | 7 points |
| Max per open show | 1 point per show |
| Application deadline | Up to 6 months after qualification date |
| Title | "JW" after dog's name |
| Stud Book | Automatically awarded stud book number |

### 11.5 Show Certificate of Excellence (ShCEx)

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Age | 18+ months |
| Points needed | 50 from open shows |
| Minimum from groups | 5 points |
| Restriction | Cannot claim same points toward both ShCM and ShCEx |

### 11.6 Veteran Warrant (VW)

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Age | 7+ years (no maximum) |
| Points needed | 25 from AV veteran classes at open shows only (not breed-specific veteran) |
| Points scale | 1st = 4, 2nd = 3, 3rd = 2, 4th = 1 |
| Max per show | 1 claim per show |
| Title | "VW" after dog's name |

### 11.7 Competition Structure at Championship Shows

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Breed classes** | Judge individual classes within breed; unbeaten class winners compete for Best Dog / Best Bitch |
| **Best of Breed** | Judge selects from Best Dog and Best Bitch |
| **Group** | BOB winners in each of 7 groups compete under different judge; placed 1st-4th |
| **Best in Show** | 7 group winners compete under another judge for BIS and RBIS |

**Best in Show scheduling (effective 2025):** BIS may now follow Best Puppy in Show and Best Veteran in Show (previously had to precede them).

### 11.8 Best Veteran in Show (NEW — effective 2026)

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Definition | Veteran = 7+ years on first day of show |
| Best Veteran in Breed | May be declared from veterans entered in breed classes |
| Best Veteran in Group | Selected from: Best Veteran in Breed + Best Veteran from AVNSC + Best Veteran from AV IBR in each group |
| Best Veteran in Show | Selected from Best Veteran in Group winners |
| Discretionary | Where BOB/BOG/BIS winner is a veteran, judge may at discretion award Best Veteran in Breed/Group/Show |

**Source:** 2026 amendments PDF
**Applicable:** All shows scheduling Best Veteran competition

### 11.9 Crufts Qualification

Key routes to qualify for Crufts (using 2026 as example):

| Route | Qualification |
|-------|--------------|
| Champion/Show Champion status | Lifetime qualification |
| Stud book number holder | Lifetime qualification |
| Championship show class placement | 1st/2nd/3rd in specified classes within qualifying period |
| Crufts previous year winner | 1st prize in any breed class at Crufts previous year |
| Premier Open Show | 1st-4th in Group or Puppy Group |
| Open show | BIS, RBIS, or Best Puppy in Show at general/group open shows |
| Junior Warrant | Qualifies for Crufts for life via stud book number |

**Crufts entry restrictions:**
- Docked dogs: not permitted
- Cropped ears: ineligible
- Bulldogs/French Bulldogs/Pugs: require valid Respiratory Function Grading (RFG) assessment within 24 months

**Source:** https://www.royalkennelclub.com/about-us/resources/media-centre/2025/may/crufts-2026-qualifications-for-entry-of-dogs/
**Applicable:** Crufts

## Appendix C: Ring Procedures (for exhibitors)

| Pattern | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Triangle** | Start in front of judge, move diagonally right, turn left at corner, follow back edge, turn left again, return diagonally to judge |
| **Straight up and down** | Position in front of judge, move to ring end, turn, return straight |
| **Circle** | Move counterclockwise along ring edge, one or more circuits |

**Placement structure:** Usually 5 placings (1st, 2nd, 3rd, Reserve, VHC). Judges may create shortlists ("cuts"). Critiques published for 1st and 2nd (sometimes 3rd).
